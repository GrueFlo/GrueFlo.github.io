My Story

As you can see through The Blood Moon artwork on the right I'm full of creativity. I used to study Computer Science, but then changed direction to Interactive Media Design.

With this field of study I really have the feeling, that all my creative dreams are achievable. Even my craziest one - Creating an Interactive Multimedia Society on Mars.

But I also want to change this world for the better. Global Warming, Neurosciences and Politics are also themes, that catch my interest.

In conclusion all these dreams only happen if I start being the change I want to see in this world.

Or in Elon Musks Words: “I could either watch it happen or be a part of it.”
