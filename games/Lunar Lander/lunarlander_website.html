<< This is Mars Lander – A game I developed in Creative Coding 1 under the advisement of Garrit Shaap >>

This is how the Game looks 

It’s a Minigame with the goal to land safely on the Mars plattform, while you’re falling from a random position to the ground.

To make the SpaceShip fall slower, press W!

To fly sideways press D or A, but remember that you’re still falling!

If you landed perfectly on the given plattform – You Win!

If you can’t reach the plattform – You Loose!

To reset the Game – Press S
